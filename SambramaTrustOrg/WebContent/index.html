What We Do

We at Sambhrama Charitable Trust believe in immediate action, innovation and the necessity for transformation, within the world and our own organization. We are explicable to the people we humbly serve, transparently sharing our results, stories and lessons.

Fundraising

It is a well-researched fact that we tend to be happy when we spend on things that make others happy rather than spend on things that give us comfort. This fact has been proven right once again by the donors who have already made a difference. Our Fundraising projects are not only focused on getting the funds for the right purpose but also to give the donor a sense of happiness and belonging in the good deed we do collectively. There is a huge magnitude of requirement that we pose from time to time, and also we will have to look for someone generous and kind like you, who are ready to participate selflessly for a better cause. Major requirement we have to provide the needy are clothes, provision, toiletries, medicines, books, sponsors for education and a lot more to the list.

Health camps and Blood Donation Camps


Health care is a liberty that not everyone is able to attain at the moment. We were able to identify that heath awareness and camps are also one of the major initiatives that are required. With a well-supported team of Doctors and health specialists, we are always ready to take up the programs, Medical camps and Blood donations camps are done on regular intervals in association with reputed health organisations.

Educational Events/ Awareness programs

Being the pioneers of a 21st century it is very essential to bring the awareness of kindness, compassion and empathy to the young minds of the society, for which education plays a major role. We conduct awareness programs relating to child safety, child abuse and child rights through various educational institutions. Our team of enthusiastic educationists spearhead these programs to the underprivileged children.

Environmental Activities

Respect towards Nature is equally important like the respect towards the country. Environmental hazards that we pose now is an alarming factor to the problems that is to come in future. Awareness programs alone cannot bring change. It is simple practical solutions that can bring about the change. We at SCT are committed to educate society to follow sustainable environmental activities.

Basic Upliftment of the underprivileged

The situation of the underprivileged in our society implies that people are not able to fully utilise their capabilities and talents to fulfil their goals. So, we at SCT try to help underprivileged people realise their talents and provide them with basic requirements to utilise their potential which helps them to have a better future.

 

Why We Do It

We believe "Collectively, we can unite and bring in a powerful change in the world. " "Coming together is beginning, keeping together is Progress and Working together is Success."

Mission

We work to save lives, defeat poverty and achieve social justice. Our focus is on the economic and educational enhancement of the disadvantaged section of the society.

Vision

To see the world through the eyes of the needy and work towards their fulfilment.
